How WashTrac Works

WashTrac keeps the employee experience simple while the controller handles real-time wash operation, safety inputs, queue behavior, activity logging, and cloud synchronization behind the scenes.

Assign Access

Managers assign secure 4-digit codes to employees, departments, vendors, or temporary users from the web platform.

Start the Wash

Users enter their code at the WashTrac terminal. The local controller validates access and starts the wash through the connected equipment interface.

Control and Verify Operation

WashTrac monitors real wash status, manages queued activations, records the outcome, and maintains a complete activity trail.

Sync, Review, and Report

Activity synchronizes to the cloud for dashboards, billing totals, CSV exports, scheduled reports, and multi-site management.
