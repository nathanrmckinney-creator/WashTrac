Relay Output Assignments

Configure Relay 1 as the wash-start output and Relays 2–6 as dry contact outputs. Relay 1–4 are hard-wired normally open; Relay 5–6 are hard-wired normally closed. Default Relay 1 assignment is Wash Trigger, off delay, 1 second.

Input Configuration

Configure all six ESP32 inputs. Input 1 remains assigned to Wash Busy and Input 2 remains assigned to E-Stop. Both remain enabled, but their electrical logic may be inverted. Factory-default E-Stop behavior is fail-safe: voltage present is healthy and loss of voltage activates E-Stop.
For bench testing with no E-Stop voltage present, enable Invert Logic on Input 2. Restore the production fail-safe setting before deployment unless the site wiring specifically requires reversed logic.
