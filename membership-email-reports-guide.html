Membership Reporting Guide

Membership Email Reports

Use WashTrac membership email reports to send member totals, monthly washes, revenue, expiration status, and detailed membership activity to managers automatically.

Membership Report Overview

Membership reports summarize the current membership program for the selected account or site.

  • Current members
  • Expired members
  • Members expiring this month
  • Membership washes this month
  • Monthly membership revenue
  • Average washes per member

Email Membership Report

Send a membership report directly from the Membership page when a manager needs an immediate update.

  • Open the Membership page
  • Confirm the correct site or account is selected
  • Enter the recipient email address
  • Click Email Membership Report
Manual membership email reports use the same membership data shown on the Membership page.

Scheduled Membership Reports

Scheduled membership reports can be sent automatically on a recurring basis.

  • Choose the report recipient
  • Select the frequency
  • Set the send time
  • Save the schedule
Scheduled membership reports are separate from normal wash activity reports and only include membership data.

Report Details Included

Each membership email report includes a member detail table for review and accountability.

  • Name
  • License plate
  • Membership code
  • Daily wash limit
  • Monthly price
  • Washes this month
  • Last wash
  • Expiration date
  • Status

Membership Expiration Tracking

Membership reports help identify which members are current, expiring soon, or expired.

  • Current memberships remain active and usable
  • Expiring this month highlights memberships needing attention
  • Expired or inactive members are separated for review

CSV Download vs Email Report

WashTrac supports both quick downloads and emailed reports depending on the workflow.

  • Use CSV download when exporting data for spreadsheets
  • Use manual email when sending a one-time report
  • Use scheduled email reports for recurring management updates

Best Practices

Keep membership reporting accurate by keeping member records up to date.

  • Update expired memberships when they renew
  • Verify monthly prices are correct
  • Review members with unusually high wash counts
  • Use scheduled reports for monthly billing review

Membership Reporting Made Simple

WashTrac membership reports keep membership revenue, usage, and expiration status organized so managers can review performance without searching through individual records.
