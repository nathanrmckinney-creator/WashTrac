Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2026

WashTrac (“WashTrac,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the WashTrac website, mobile application, and related services.

Information We Collect

WashTrac may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information, such as name, email address, and login credentials.
  • Site and organization information, including car wash locations, departments, and user permissions.
  • Wash activity data, including access codes, usage history, timestamps, department assignments, and membership activity.
  • Device and system information, including device status, connectivity status, command logs, and maintenance settings.
  • Technical information, such as browser type, app version, device type, and basic diagnostic data used to maintain service reliability.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and operate the WashTrac service.
  • Authenticate users and manage account access.
  • Track wash activity, access code usage, memberships, and reports.
  • Provide monitoring, maintenance, and administrative tools.
  • Improve security, reliability, and performance.
  • Communicate important service-related information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Information may be shared only when necessary to operate WashTrac, comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, or provide services through trusted infrastructure providers.

Data Storage and Security

WashTrac uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide WashTrac services, maintain records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

User Choices

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their information by contacting us. Some records may need to be retained for legitimate business, security, accounting, or legal purposes.

Children’s Privacy

WashTrac is intended for business and commercial use. It is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Services

WashTrac may rely on third-party service providers for hosting, authentication, database storage, email delivery, app distribution, analytics, or infrastructure. These providers may process limited information only as needed to support WashTrac services.

Mobile App

The WashTrac mobile application provides access to the WashTrac web service. The app may use internet access, local storage, and device functionality necessary to display and operate WashTrac.
