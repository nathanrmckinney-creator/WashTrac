Reporting Guide

Reports & Audit Logs

Use WashTrac reporting tools to analyze usage, export data, schedule email reports, and maintain a complete audit trail.

Audit Trail Overview

Every keypad event is recorded to create a complete and searchable history.

  • Successful washes
  • Denied access attempts
  • Date and time of each event
  • Code, name, department, and site

Reports Dashboard

View high-level statistics and visual charts showing wash activity trends.

  • Total washes
  • Denied attempts
  • Top wash codes
  • Usage by time of day

Filtering Data

Narrow report results to focus on specific information.

  • Select a date range
  • Filter by site
  • Filter by code, user, or department

Scheduled Email Reports

Automatically send reports to managers and administrators.

  • Choose recipients
  • Select report frequency
  • Set start and end dates
Reports can be delivered automatically to one or more email addresses on a recurring schedule.

Exporting Data

Download activity data for analysis in spreadsheets or accounting systems.

  • Export CSV files
  • Share audit data with management
  • Archive records for compliance

Top Wash Codes

Identify the most frequently used codes and associated users.

  • Rank code usage
  • Display names next to codes
  • Spot unusual activity

Wash Activity by Time

Understand the busiest periods during the day.

  • 24-hour usage chart
  • Average activity by time of day
  • Trend analysis

Complete Accountability

WashTrac reporting tools provide a permanent audit trail and actionable insights to help you control costs and improve accountability.
